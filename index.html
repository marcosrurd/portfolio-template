About Me

I’m a graphic designer with a strong focus on visual storytelling, editorial design, and audiovisual projects. I enjoy developing ideas from concept to execution, exploring how design can communicate mood, narrative, and intention.

I’m particularly interested in creative and art direction, where concept, visual language, and emotion come together. My goal is to keep building a personal approach to design while collaborating on projects that value creativity, experimentation, and thoughtful visual expression.
